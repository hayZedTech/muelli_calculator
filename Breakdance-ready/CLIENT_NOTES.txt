Breakdance-ready package

This folder is a standalone version of the Muelli Calculator page for client use.

What to do:
- Copy the whole folder to the desired project location.
- Use it as a static page or embed it in your Breakdance setup.
- If needed, replace the sample WhatsApp number in index.html.

Important:
- The calculator works directly in the browser.
- No backend is required for the demo version.
- The form opens WhatsApp or email when submitted.
